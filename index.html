How do I get to Imilchil and G√ģte NOMADE?

Imilchil is accessible by road from Marrakech (approx. 5 hours) or Er Rachidia (3.5 hours). The route is incredibly scenic through the High Atlas mountains. We highly recommend arriving during daylight hours.

What is the best time to visit Imilchil?

Spring (April to June) and Autumn (September to November) offer the best weather for trekking and exploring. September is especially popular for the legendary Moussem des Fian√ßailles (Marriage Festival).

Is breakfast included in the room price?

Yes, our traditional Amazigh breakfast (including Msemen, Amlou, and mint tea) is included in all room rates to start your mountain day right.

Our Story

Where the
Mountains Breathe

Gîte NOMADE chez Moulay is a family-run auberge nestled in Imilchil — Morocco's legendary mountain village at 2,119 metres in the High Atlas. We open our doors as Amazigh hosts have always welcomed travelers: with mint tea, a warm hearth, and stories older than the trails themselves.
